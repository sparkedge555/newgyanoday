A Beacon of Education Since 2004

New Gyanoday English School has stood as a pillar of quality education in the Tribeniganj block of Supaul district, Bihar. Our school follows the CBSE Pattern, delivering a holistic curriculum from Nursery through Class 10.

We are proud to prepare students for prestigious national examinations including Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sainik School, Simultala Awasiya Vidyalaya, and BHU entrance exams — building a strong academic foundation with disciplined mentoring.

World-Class Facilities

Every resource you need for a complete education experience.

11 Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms designed for focused learning and collaborative activities.

Computer Lab

10 computers with Computer-Aided Learning (CAL) to prepare students for the digital world.

Library

A well-stocked library with over 5,000 books across academic and general knowledge domains.

Hostel

Separate, secure, and comfortable hostel facilities for boys and girls with 24/7 supervision.

Playground

A dedicated playground to encourage physical fitness, sportsmanship, and team spirit.

Electricity & Power

Uninterrupted electricity supply ensures a smooth, comfortable learning environment year-round.

Drinking Water

Clean, safe drinking water available through hand pumps across the school campus.

Ramps for Disabled

Specially designed ramps ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for differently-abled students.

Sanitization

4 boys' toilets and 2 girls' toilets maintained with proper hygiene and sanitation standards.
